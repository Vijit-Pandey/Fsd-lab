Hotels in Pune

  • The Westin Pune Koregaon Park: Offers a luxurious stay with its spacious rooms, excellent service, and prime location near the river.
  • JW Marriott Hotel Pune: Known for its elegant accommodations, outstanding amenities, and convenient location in the heart of the city.
  • Conrad Pune: Features sophisticated design, a full-service spa, and multiple dining options, providing a five-star experience.
  • Hyatt Regency Pune & Residences: Offers a comfortable stay with modern rooms, an indoor pool, and wellness facilities.
  • The O Hotel: A boutique hotel with stylish rooms, a rooftop pool, and fine dining options, located in the fashionable Koregaon Park neighborhood.

Pune hosts a variety of hotels ranging from luxury to budget-friendly options, catering to the needs of both business and leisure travelers. These hotels are known for their hospitality and the quality of their services, making your stay in Pune a memorable one.
