Pune Public Transport

  • Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Ltd (PMPML): The city's bus service, providing extensive coverage across Pune and its suburbs.
  • Pune Metro: A rapid transit system under construction aimed at easing the traffic congestion in the city.
  • Auto Rickshaws: A common mode of transport for short to medium distances within the city.
  • Ola and Uber: App-based cab services are widely used for convenient city and outstation travel.

Pune's public transport system is undergoing significant changes with the introduction of the Metro, which is expected to greatly improve connectivity within the city. The PMPML buses also serve as a lifeline for daily commuters, connecting various parts of Pune and its neighboring areas.

The city's transport system is complemented by the availability of app-based cab services and traditional auto rickshaws, making it easier for residents and visitors to navigate Pune.
